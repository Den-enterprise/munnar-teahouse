About us

Munnar Tea House is a homegrown, woman-owned enterprise born from the heart of Kerala.
We bring you the finest tea, flavoured teas, whole spices, pickles, honey and other homemade savory delights, carefully sourced from the lush Western Ghats and beyond.
Though our roots lie in a family kitchen, every product is crafted and packed with professional market standards, ensuring freshness, purity, and authenticity with every box.
What makes us special goes beyond flavor—our brand is built on compassion. With every Spicefulness Tea Box you purchase, ₹2 is donated to support cancer warriors, turning your everyday cup of tea into a gesture of care and hope.
At Munnar Tea House and Spicefulness, we don’t just deliver taste—we deliver a story of tradition, quality, and kindness.
